XEN

XEN is a queer multidisciplinary artist based
in Los Angeles. Their work explores
the intersection of nature and technology
through the lens of queer identity
and sexuality. By employing 3D modeling,
sculpture, music experimentation,
and video performance, XEN deconstructs
societal taboos, reconstructing them
into works that question
one's relation to themself.
